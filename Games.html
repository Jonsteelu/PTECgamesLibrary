Arrow Assault

Created by Jonathan

In Arrow Assault you play as an archer who fights off waves of skeletons.
Use the space bar to shoot your bow, killing the skeletons.
Collect the bones and skulls the skeletons drop to purchase items from the shop.
After purchasing "arrow barage" Use E to fire a barage of arrows quickly.
Try to survive as long as possible by shooting skeletons,
buying health, shields, arrow barages, and improving your reload speed.
